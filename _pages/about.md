I am a software engineer based in Dhaka, Bangladesh, with over two years of experience 
building backend systems, industrial automation pipelines, and computer vision solutions 
for real world environments.

My work spans building automation infrastructure for real world industrial workflows and developing computer vision systems for factory environments. I have worked on systems that eliminate repetitive manual processes at scale, from data extraction and booking pipelines to export monitoring with live dashboards. More recently my focus has shifted toward visual intelligence, working on real time multi camera inference pipelines that run detection models and surface operational insights continuously. I am drawn to problems where engineering precision translates directly into real world reliability.

I am now transitioning toward research, with a growing interest in learning based methods 
applied to visual understanding. My current work involves building fish detection models 
and curating datasets for aquatic species identification, with a planned research 
publication underway.

I hold a B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from 
[BRAC University](https://www.bracu.ac.bd) and work full time as a Software Engineer 
at [AlterSense Limited](https://www.altersense.com).
